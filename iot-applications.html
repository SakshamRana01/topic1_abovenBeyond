ACTIVITY 2 :- (Smart Buildings)

Activity 2 is all about the smart building , in which we discussed -> what does smart mean in technology
terminology , also understood the difference between a smart device and a simple device . We also focused on the main features need for the building to be smart , and also gone through some of the smart building scenarios .

WHAT ARE SMART BUILDINGS ?

Something that operates effectively and integrate into a communication network while concealing all complexity from the user to minimise human effort.

What are some Smart-building scenarios?

  • Voice control operation in room.
  • Retina recognition door locks.
  • AI powered assistance.
  • It employs preventative and predictive maintenance strategies and uses real-time field data to spot problems.
